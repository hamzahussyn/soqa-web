SOQA BOT - Powered with AI


The problem

University professors face countless queries from the students. From the mundane to the complex, the faculty is inundated with questions which tend to take away useful time and hinder the learning process.

SOQA the solution

We are providing an AI based solution by using Amazon and open GL tools to create a chatbot based off faculty course syllabus. Different tiers of questions and queries have been created which are answered by a combination of tools.

An incredibly fast learner

Learning new things and reading bulk of text could be tire some. Thats where SOQA comes to play. It can read from the textual documents and learn the facts, core ideas, and abstract concepts to generate intelligent answers to your questions!

Be more productive in learning by letting SOQA do the labourious reading.

Ask away!

Instead of asking quesions through traditional search engines and getting little to no accurate answers, ask them from SOQA, because SOQA answers by learning from your course material unlike search engines which use indexed Wikipedia pages.
